Core Research

PhD Thesis

Techno-environ-economic assessment of waste algae to biogas for GHG mitigation from eutrophic lakes

This work aims to accurately measure and assess GHG dynamics of eutrophicated lakes along with energy recovery and surface water flux.

Key Project

Waste algae to biogas for clean energy and environment

Focused on evaluating the prospects of converting waste algae into biogas as a sustainable solution for clean energy and environmental management.
