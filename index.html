About Me

I'm Amit, a passionate researcher dedicated to addressing environmental challenges through scientific innovation. My interest in renewable energy began during my undergraduate studies and has since grown into a full-fledged research career focused on the intersection of waste management, bioenergy, and climate change mitigation.

My PhD work at the Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee, involves a deep dive into the greenhouse gas dynamics of eutrophic lakes. I'm exploring how harnessing waste algae for biogas can not only provide a clean energy source but also restore aquatic ecosystems.
