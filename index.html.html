I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. as a Prime Minister's Research Fellow (PMRF) in the Hydro and Renewable Energy Department (HRED) at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee. Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Flux Pathways

Eutrophic Lakes & Ponds Dynamics

Eutrophicated water bodies recharged with wastewater treatment plant (STP) effluent trigger immense biological activities. High phosphate and nitrate concentrations promote microalgal blooms.

  • Solar Ingress: Fuels high photosynthetic rates.
  • Diffusive Fluxes: Steady surface emission of dissolved $CO_2$ & $CH_4$.
  • Ebullition: Bubble pathways carrying benthic methanogenesis $CH_4$ direct to the atmosphere.

Floating Chamber Design & Flux Estimation

Direct Field Measuring Setup

For direct, field-scale evaluation of methane ($CH_4$) and carbon dioxide ($CO_2$) emissions across water-air boundaries, customized floating chambers are engineered.

The chamber is enclosed over the surface with an airtight seal. An embedded internal micro-vent and temperature logger feed gas concentration increases directly via flexible polymer tubes to a portable gas analyzer.

Flux Equation: $F = \frac{V}{A} \cdot \frac{dC}{dt} \cdot \frac{P \cdot M}{R \cdot T}$ where $dC/dt$ is the gas accumulation slope.

Circular Power-to-Methane (P2M) Bio-refinery

Integrated Clean Energy Framework

This futuristic loop combines wastewater bioremediation with active climate action. Algal blooms are systematically harvested, reducing in situ carbon emissions.

The harvested biomass enters an Anaerobic Digester (AD). The resultant biogas ($60\%\, CH_4$, $40\%\, CO_2$) is upgraded biologically inside the digester using Renewable Green Hydrogen ($H_2$) sourced from solar/water electrolysis. This drives Sabatier methanation, yielding pure pipeline-grade biomethane ($95\%\, CH_4$).

Principal Research Objectives

01

GHG Assessments in Eutrophic Systems

Conducting intensive seasonal monitoring of diffusive and ebullitive methane ($CH_4$) and carbon dioxide ($CO_2$) emissions across diverse water networks (like the Hauz Khas Pond, South Delhi) influenced by secondary and tertiary treated STP domestic effluent discharges.

02

Algal Harvesting for Decarbonization

Quantifying hyper-eutrophicated standing biomass volumes and deploying mechanical/nature-based Harvesting Solutions to strip nutrients and carbon content, lowering in-situ environmental footprint and gas emissions.

03

Optimizing Biogas Output (Anaerobic Digestion)

Executing Biochemical Methane Potential (BMP) test suites on harvested algal species to discover competitive methane yields, identifying operational parameters like volatile solids, organic loading rates, and temperatures.

04

Power-to-Gas (P2M) Integration

Simulating in-situ upgrading of AD biogas using externally produced green hydrogen, converting toxic/excess $CO_2$ biologically into pipeline quality $CH_4$ gas (>95%), realizing circular energy recovery loops.
